Welcome to ACE Museum

ACE Museum is the world's first museum dedicated to the history, evolution, and culture of tennis rackets. From wooden classics used in the early 1900s to the carbon fiber frames of today's champions, our collection spans over a century of innovation, design, and athletic achievement.

About ACE Museum

ACE Museum houses over 300 rackets from every era of tennis, including rare frames used by Grand Slam champions. Our interactive exhibits let you feel the difference between generations of racket technology.

Founded in 2020, ACE Museum started as a private collection and grew into a public institution celebrating the craft behind the sport. Beyond rackets, we explore string technology, grip design, and how engineering has shaped the modern game. We host monthly talks with racket designers, stringing workshops, and family-friendly events. Whether you are a competitive player or just curious about the sport, ACE Museum has something for you.
